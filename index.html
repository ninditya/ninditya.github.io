about

Background & skills

I'm Ninditya Salma Nur Aini, based in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. My background spans AI engineering, data science, and QA.

My career started in chatbot development at Infomedia (Telkom Group), designing rule-based conversation flows serving thousands of daily users. I then moved to Flip as a Chatbot QA Analyst, evaluating LLM-based chatbots with RAG frameworks and building the exact AI quality assessment skills the industry now urgently needs.

Parallel to that, I deepened formal data science training through Binar Academy and the IDCamp Expert track (Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison), building end-to-end pipelines from raw API ingestion to monitored, production-grade deployments with MLflow, Docker, and Grafana.

What makes my profile unusual: I've seen what happens to AI systems after they ship — fallback rates, hallucinations, drift, CSAT regressions. That perspective shapes how I build models and how I test them. QA isn't just a safety net for me — it's a core competency woven into every stage of development.
