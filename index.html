about

Background & skills

I'm Ninditya Salma Nur Aini, a Data Scientist and ML Engineer based in Yogyakarta, Indonesia with 3.5+ years of production AI experience.

My career started in chatbot development at Infomedia (Telkom Group), designing rule-based conversation flows serving thousands of daily users. I then moved to Flip to evaluate LLM-based chatbots with RAG frameworks — building the exact AI quality assessment skills that the industry now urgently needs as generative AI moves into production.

Parallel to that, I deepened formal data science training through Binar Academy and the IDCamp Expert track (Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison), building end-to-end pipelines from raw API ingestion through ML modelling to monitored, production-grade deployments with MLflow, Docker, and Grafana.

What makes my profile unusual: I've seen what happens to AI systems after they ship — fallback rates, hallucinations, drift, CSAT regressions. That perspective shapes how I build models, not just how I evaluate them.

Currently deepening expertise in LLM application development and MLOps. Open to Data Scientist, ML Engineer, and Conversational AI Engineer roles — remote, hybrid, or based in Yogyakarta/Jakarta.
